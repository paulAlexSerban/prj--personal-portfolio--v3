## Problem

Invoice financing is slow, opaque, and intermediary-heavy. We set out to prove a decentralised settlement flow could be built to a production-viable standard under extreme time pressure.

## Approach

- **Led a team of 4 engineers** through full-stack delivery in a single 48-hour sprint.
- **Architected the contract layer** in Move/Rust on the Sui blockchain, deployed to the Sui Foundation testnet.
- **Shipped the web app** in Next.js on Vercel, wired directly to on-chain settlement.

## Outcome

**Won 1st place against 50+ international teams** with a working, production-viable decentralised application delivered end-to-end in 48 hours.
